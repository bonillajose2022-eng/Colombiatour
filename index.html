What do you know about Colombia?

Colombia is a country in South America. It is known for coffee, flowers, and friendly people!

🏙️

Do you know any cities in Colombia?

Bogotá is the capital city. Medellín, Cartagena, and Cali are also very famous!
Tap to think! 💭
🦜

What animals can you find in Colombia?

Colombia has jaguars, pink dolphins, toucans, butterflies, and the famous Andean condor!
Tap to think! 💭
🍲

What Colombian food do you know?

Arepa, bandeja paisa, empanadas, and hot chocolate are very popular Colombian foods!
